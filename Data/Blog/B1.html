How I created my first site

5:33PM, 7th Aug 2021

I always dreamed of having my own personal site. And finally after procrastinating for two years, I decided that I will make it. It took me 10 days from writing the first line of code till I hosted it. It was a fun journey to reach here. So I will show the insides of it here.

What language do I use?

I primarily used HTML, CSS and javascript for this site. Whole front end you see is in HTML and CSS for which code is available on my github.

Where do I learn?

For whatever design came to my mind, I just googled the topic and step by step I constructed this site.

How much time did it take to complete it?

I had lower level proficiency in both HTML & CSS, so I had to google for many things. It took around 20 hours to get the final result.

What next?

I have plans to make this super user friendly, so I will release updates soon. For any suggestions, I will attach a google form at the bottom.
